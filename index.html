General Server Rules

1

Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect regardless of their role, experience level, or background. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

2

No Cheating or Exploiting

Use of cheats, hacks, exploits, or any third-party tools to gain an unfair advantage will result in a permanent ban without warning.

3

Staff Decisions Are Final

Respect our decisions even when harsh. If you disagree with a decision, you may appeal through the proper channels on our Discord.

4

One Character Per Person

You may create multiple characters, but only one character can be active at a time. Character switching to avoid RP consequences is prohibited.

5

Appropriate Character Names

Character names must be realistic and appropriate. No offensive, celebrity, or meme names are allowed.

6

No Combat Logging

Disconnecting to avoid roleplay situations, especially during combat or police interactions, is prohibited.

7

Report Bugs

If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately. Abuse of bugs for personal gain is prohibited.

Roleplay Guidelines

1

Value Your Life

Always act as if your character values their life. Don't make unrealistic decisions that put your character in unnecessary danger.

2

No Powergaming

Don't force actions on other players or create scenarios where others have no choice or chance to respond.

3

No Metagaming

Using information your character wouldn't know in-game (from Discord, streams, etc.) is strictly prohibited.

4

Realistic Roleplay

Keep your actions realistic and consistent with your character's background and abilities.

5

No Breaking Character

Stay in character at all times during roleplay interactions. Use appropriate channels for OOC communication.

Police & Criminal Rules

1

Police Response

Police must respond appropriately to the level of crime. Not every situation requires maximum force.

2

Criminal Activity

All criminal activities must have proper roleplay and planning. Random violence without roleplay context is prohibited.

3

Robbery Cooldowns

Respect the cooldown periods between robberies and other major criminal activities.

4

Gang Activity

Gang wars and territory disputes must be approved by staff and have proper roleplay context.

Vehicles & Properties Rules

1

Realistic Driving

Drive realistically at all times. No excessive speeding in city areas or reckless driving that endangers others.

2

Vehicle Ownership

Only drive vehicles your character owns or has permission to use. Stealing vehicles without proper roleplay is prohibited.

3

Property Rules

Respect property boundaries and ownership. Entering private property without permission requires proper roleplay.

Communication Rules

1

Clear Communication

Communicate clearly and respectfully with other players. Avoid using excessive slang or unclear language.

2

No OOC in IC

Keep out-of-character communication in appropriate channels. Don't break character during roleplay situations.

3

Respect Voice Chat

Keep voice chat clean and roleplay-focused. Avoid background noise and unrelated conversations.

Rule Enforcement

All rules are strictly enforced by our moderation team. Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity. We reserve the right to update these rules at any time.
